### Selectors

-   Universal Selector `* {}`
-   Type Selector `h1, h2 ,h3 {}`
-   Child Selector `ul > li {}`
-   Descendant Selector `p a {}`
-   Class Selector .class `{}`
-   ID Selector `#id {}`
-   Adjacent Sibling Selector `h1 + p {}`
-   General Sibling Selector `h1 ~ p {}`
-   Attribute Selector `[attribute="SomeValue"] {}`
