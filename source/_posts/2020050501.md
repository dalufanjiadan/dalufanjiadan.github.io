### How to convert List<Integer> to int[] in Java?

```java
int[] array = list.stream().mapToInt(i->i).toArray();
```

---

### How to convert int[] into List<Integer> in Java?

```java
int[] ints = {1,2,3};
List<Integer> list = Arrays.stream(ints).boxed().collect(Collectors.toList());
```
